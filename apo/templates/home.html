What is Alpha Phi Omega?

Alpha Phi Omega is a gender-inclusive national

service fraternity affiliated and founded on Boy

Scout Principles. As an organization, we strive

to better our community, campus, country, and

beyond through serving others and volunteerism,

while promoting ideals of leadership, friendship,

and service amongst members. First established

on Lafayette's College campus (PA), in 1925,

membership has since grown to over 525,000

members and 325 chapters nationwide!
